About Us

At Now Boarding, we're passionate about working with pets. We're proud of the personalized services we offer and the ways we prioritize each guest's health and happiness.

Now Boarding reopened in February 2021 under new ownership! Although we’re no longer associated with Animal Humane Society, our goal is to continue the great experience you and your pet have come to expect from a stay at Now Boarding.

Conveniently located just minutes from the Minneapolis/St. Paul International Airport, we’re proud to have 23,000 super-clean square feet dedicated to the care and comfort of dogs.

Every member of our carefully chosen staff is here because they're passionate about working with pets. Our team members are parents to 30 dogs. We treat every animal that walks through our doors as though they’re our own pets.
